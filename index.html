I am a web developer for social impact

My mission is to support and empower social entrepreneurs from end to end. I help them by building meaningful digital products and services in different, new ways that can solve fast-growing challenges.

I started working as a freelance web developer while studying computer science at the University of Trento. Since then I have pursued my master degree in Software and Service Architectures (EIT Digital) and worked together with ambitious companies and individuals around the world to cultivate positive change.

I now combine new technologies and frameworks with agile methodology and team up with a small tribe of passionate freelancers.

Amongst others, I have helped a culinary social enterprise for female refugees in the UK, supported a worldwide project in the refugees economy, and contributed to set up an inclusive global lunch program for organizations, of which I’m the co-founder.

My office fits my suitcase, which allows me to work remote and meet my ambitious clients all over the world. I believe that acting responsibly and positively inspires others to do the same; together we will bring the change we want to see – it all starts with you. If you want to bring your project forward, let’s chat.

Services

My services are available as low-risk, fixed-price packages. Feel free to ask for guidance — I’ll show you around and explain all possible options. Should you need a custom solution, please get in touch and we’ll figure out the right way to work together.

One Page Design

Do you have an event, a series of webinars, or a campaign you want to share with the world? I’ll design a beautiful and effective landing page that captures the mission of your endeavor while conveying all the information needed in one single page.

One Page design packages starts at €900. Get in touch for details.

Web Development

I build fast, interactive websites for your organization by using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This also may include integrating a CMS (mostly Wordpress). The basic package includes only website development. However, if you need art direction, branding, illustrations, and photography, I’ll engage my talented team of freelancers.

Web Development packages starts at €3000. Get in touch for details.

Wordpress Maintenance

You've got an successful website, but nobody to take care of it? I'll help you keep it up and running safe. At a fixed monthly rate, I'll take care of monitoring, security updates, and small bug fixes so you can focus on growing your app.

Wordpress Maintenance packages starts at €1000/month. Get in touch for details.

WE Factory

WE Factory is a workplace food design and research studio founded by Veronica Fossa. WE Factory is dedicated to providing rich and relevant content and products supporting people at work. By combining the power of tools, food, location, and community they help create work environments in which people reconnect with each other and grow with a sense of purpose.

I’ve been their web designer since they launched in 2014, and over the course of three years I designed three versions of the website. The purpose of this new version was to convey their renewed mission and scope of work. The outcome is a playful, modern, and fresh design with flexible and simple functionality to inspire and delight the user.

Stories on our Plate

Stories On Our Plate (SOOP) is an organisation that uses food and storytelling to challenge the negative attitudes towards cooks with refugee and migrant backgrounds living in the United Kingdom.

SOOP needed an elegant and modern website that would convey the purpose of their activities and showcase both their thriving community of chefs and the growing list of supper clubs. With Clara Ernst, who designed the new logo, I constructed a website that looks fresh, contemporary, and highly appealing.

Halfway between the Adriatic Sea, Garda Lake, and the Dolomites, Mezzavia is an unexpected gem. A 3-bedroom apartment in Arsiero, a town at the foothills of the Prealps in Veneto.
